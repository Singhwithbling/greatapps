Docksy

Turn a spare iPhone or iPad into a deck of glass buttons that run things on your Mac with one tap.

How it works

Three taps to set up

1

Install on your Mac

Download the free Docksy app for Mac and open it. Grant it permission to run shortcuts, and leave it running.

2

Open it on your phone

Get Docksy from the App Store on a spare iPhone or iPad and allow Local Network access so it can find your Mac.

3

Tap and go

On the same Wi-Fi, your Mac shows up automatically. Build your deck of buttons and start tapping.

💡

What you need: a Mac running the free Docksy app, an iPhone or iPad, and both on the same local Wi-Fi network. Everything stays on your own devices. There is no account and nothing is sent to the cloud.

What you can do

Put anything on a button

⌨️

Keyboard shortcuts

Copy, paste, or any combo you choose. Just press the keys to record it.

🚀

Open anything

Launch an app, a website, a file, or a folder the moment you tap.

🪟

Control windows

Close, minimize, hide, or quit. Tidy your desktop with one press.

🔊

A slider you can feel

A volume slider that buzzes in your hand as it rises. Play, pause, skip, mute.

⏱️

Timers & text

Countdown timers right on the key, and saved text snippets you reuse.

🎨

Make it yours

Any color, any icon or emoji, resizable keys, folders and pages.
